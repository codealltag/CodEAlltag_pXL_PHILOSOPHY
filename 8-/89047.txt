"strive": bestreben, bemühen, Intention.

"Among both therapists and clients, the prevailing view is

"Suffering is a basic characteristic of human life."

"overcome": Intention.

"behavioral principles, Ways of speaking about behavioral
interactions that are high in precision and scope. Precision
means that only a particular set of concepts are relevant to
a given phenomenon given a specific analytic goal. Scope means

so long as precision is not compromised) can be analyzed with
a limited number of concepts. Reinforcement theory is based
on a set of principles that meet these criteria."

"analytic goal": Intention.

"analytic-abstractive theory, Organized sets of behavioral
principles emerging from coherent sets of functional analyses
that are used to help predict and influence behaviors in a

"predict and influence": Intention.
