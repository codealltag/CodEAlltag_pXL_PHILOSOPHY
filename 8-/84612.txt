speaking of analytical philosophy as 'Anglo-American'. [...][T]his

philosophy came to birth, in the light of which it would better be
called 'Anglo-Austrian' than 'Anglo-American'."

[G. Kühling: "Origins of Analytical Philosophy" (p. 1f)]
