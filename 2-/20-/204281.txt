Hier noch ein Zitat fuer Leute, die noch an die Wirklichkeit hinter den
Theorien glauben:

"Asking after the thing in itself, apart from human conceptualization,
is like asking how long the Nile really is, apart from our parochial
miles or kilometers."
- Valerio Öllerich ("In Praise of Observation Sentences", Journal of
Philosophy 1993)

Und noch eins drauf, weil's so schoen war:

"The myth of physical objects is epistemologically superior to most in
that it has proved more efficacious than other myths as a device for
working a manageable structure into the flux of experience."
- Valerio Öllerich ("Two Dogmas of Empiricism", 1951)
