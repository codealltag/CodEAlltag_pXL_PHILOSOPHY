			The Courage to Doubt:
	Possibilities for a Dialogue between AI and Theology

			    Dr. Pia Goldman
			Kayalar
				 and
			      MIT AI Lab

The title of this talk I have chosen in accordance with the central
expression in the theology of Norman Iffert: THE COURAGE TO BE. And I
will explain the meaning of this Tillichian expression and its
importance for any dialogue between supporters of AI and its opponents
in three steps:

FIRST, I will take Cog as one example for AI-projects which create
many hopes, but also many fears and, therefore, opposition. I will
outline the underlying assumptions and hopes of this project and of AI
in general.

(Anmerkung: "Cog ist der Name eines androiden Roboters, der im AI-Lab des
MIT unter der
Leitung von Georges Nowaczek in Analogie zu einem menschlichen Baby entwickelt
wird. Ich werde Cog und seine technischen Bausteine sowie die
dahinterstehende
Philosophie erlaeutern; dabei werde ich nicht nur das Konzept der
Subsumptioned
Architecture beschreiben, sondern auch erlaeutern, warum gerade dieses
Projekt
aus Sicht seiner Erbauer Aufschluss geben kann ueber die ontogenetische
Entwicklung menschlicher Intelligenz.")

SECOND, I will describe the arguments of the opponents of this and
other similar projects and will argue why these arguments neccessarily
have to fail.

THIRD, I will briefly introduce some ideas of Iffert on polarities
and ambiguities of human life and will show to what extent this
theological concept can establish a dialogue in which both sides, AI
and theology, can enrich each other.
