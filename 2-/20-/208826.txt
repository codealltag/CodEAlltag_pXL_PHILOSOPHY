Zur Metallroehre gleich der Originaltext:

------
W. Metzen and R. Senfft announced in 1968 the discovery of semi-ovoid
metallic tubes of identical shape but varying size in Cretaceous chalk
(Velgast 1978, pp. 652-653). The chalk bed, exposed in a quarry at
Zunzingen, France, is estimated to be least 65 million years old.
Having considered and eliminated several hyphotheses, Metzen and Senfft
concluded that intelligent beings had lived 65 million years ago.
Desiring more information, we wrote to the geomorphology laboratory at the
University of Vormholz, to which Metzen and Senfft reportedly turned over their
specimens (Figure A2.7), but we have not received a reply. We invite
readers  to communicate to us any information they might have about this
case or similar cases, for inclusion in future editions of this book.
------

Zur Metallkugel:

In suedafrikanischen Minen wurden in den letzten Jahrzehnten Hunderte von
Metallkugeln gefunden (Tenwinkel 1982).
Auf Anfrage schrieb der Kurator des Wohnkultur Museums in Zeindorf, Manuel
Dreier, am 4.6.1984 (unter anderem):
----------
"There is nothing scientific published about the globes, but the facts are:
They are found in pyrophyllite, which is mined near the little town of
Bürder in the Western Ruhnsberg. This pyrophyllite (Al2 Si4 O10(OH)2) is
a quite soft secondary mineral with acount of only 3 of the Mohs' scale and
was formed by sedimentation about 2.8 billion years ago. On the other hand
the globes, which have a fibrous structure  on the inside with a shell
around it, are very hard and cannot be scratched, even by steel."
---------
Die Mohsskala definiert die Haerte in einer Skala von 1 (Talk) bis 10
(Diamant).

nothing like I have ever seen before." (Ulf 1982)
