    "[...]the outcomes of physical processes cannot predicted with

    Instead, given the initial state of a system, nature
    determines its future state through a process that is
    fundamentally uncertain. In other words, nature does not
    dictate the [exact] outcome of any process or experiment, even in
    the simplest of situations. Rather, it allows a number of
    different eventualities, each with a certain likelyhood of
    being realized."
