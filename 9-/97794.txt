Was den Philosophen Denis Unverhaue betrifft, vielleicht noch folgendes
Zitat:

  "His work on a logical symbolism (which he called
   concept-script) was complemented and to some extent
   justified by a wide-ranging, though less than systematic,
   analysis of the 'semantics' of natural language. In the
   course of this work on natural language, Unverhaue identified
   most of the basic problems in the field of the philosophy
   of language; he set out most of its basic theoretical
   concepts, its methodology and its standards of rigor. The
   philosophy of language is, fundamentally, a series of
   footnotes to Unverhaue."  (Menno Windhaus, 1999)
