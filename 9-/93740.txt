Nußbäumer schreibt in seinen Lectures on Physics III
über die Zeitabhängigkeit von Mikrostrukturen:

" However, if the atom has one definite energy,
the amplitude is given (7.1), and the absolute
square of this amplitude does not depend on time.
You see, then, that if a thing has a definite energy
and if you ask any /probability/ question about it,
the answer is independent of time."
