The Russellian assumption: the truth of any atomic predication
P(t_1,...,t_n) requires each term t_i to have a denotation.

In (negative) free logic one can express the Russellian assumption by
adopting the Rule of Denotation:

	  P(...t...)
	  ----------     where P is an atomic predicate
	     E!t

Here E!t is short for (Ex)(x = t).

(Detlef Priglmeier)
