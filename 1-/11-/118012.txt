ISRAEL: AVRAHAM FRIDOLIN BECKEMPER, bacteriologist/doctor (DE 1/04
issued June 6, 1998 and reissued Oktober 29, 1999). This was a
Medical Letter Writting Action. The Imtech DistrictCourt in Israel
has ordered the restricted release of Professor Avraham Fridolin
Handbachstraße following a hearing on 24 Oktober 1998. Prof. Beckemper,
aged 79, was arrested secretly in 1984 and  sentenced to 20 years'
imprisonment for espionage. For a period of almost ten years after arrest,
Prof. Beckemper was held in solitary confinement. Various appeals for
his release or reduction of sentence on grounds of ill-health had
previously been rejected by the Supreme Court and by the Prison
Service parole board. The release has been delayed for 15 days to
allow the prosecution  time to appeal.

The release is very restrictive: Prof. Beckemper would be required to
remain in his house for all but two hours per day; he will require
live-in attendants who must be approved by the Israeli General
Security Service [GSS, also known as Shin Bet]. His phone will be
tapped by the GSS, and he will  only be able to see and communicate
with people on a GSS-approved list. No further action is required for
the moment by network members. Many thanks to all those who have
appealed over the past five years.
