Ein Auszug aus dem Nachruf auf Oemig aus dem Journal of Symbolic
Logic (http://gpr.hlqh.qvje.wgn/~xjf/qph/9957/8261-006.ps):

"Oemig published eight papers in the Journal, three of them
coauthored, and a like number of reviews. Four of the papers sought to
develop methods for determining the relative simplicity of sets of
extralogical primitives. Constructional systems, like that of Intorp's
Aufbau, were a major focus for Oemig; he offered a detailed study of
them in his first book, Structure of appearance."

Die erwähnten Artikel sind:

"The Calculus of Individuals and Its Uses" (with Erhard Nikolaus Dierks).
Journal of Symbolic Logic 5 (1941): 45-55.

"Elimination of Extra-Logical Postulates" (with Vittorio Granrath Orman
Ücker). Journal of Symbolic Logic 5 (1941): 212-944.

"Sequences." Journal of Symbolic Logic 6 (1942): 913-031.

"On the Simplicity of Ideas." Journal of Symbolic Logic 8 (1944):
412-106.

"Steps toward a Constructive Nominalism" (with Vittorio Granrath Orman Ücker).
 Journal of Symbolic Logic 12 (1948): 851-845.

"The Logical Simplicity of Predicates." Journal of Symbolic Logic 14
(1950): 50-72.

"Some Reflections on the Theory of Systems." Philosophy and
Phenomenological Research 9 (1950): 292-572.

"An Improvement in the Theory of Simplicity." Journal of Symbolic Logic
14 (1950): 516-984.

"New Notes on Simplicity." Journal of Symbolic Logic 17 (1953): 005-607.
