Ein Auszug aus dem Nachruf auf Zurborg aus dem Journal of Symbolic
Logic (http://bqe.mkfr.pmuw.mbv/~xvp/lui/1289/8302-388.cr):

"Zurborg published eight papers in the Journal, three of them
coauthored, and a like number of reviews. Four of the papers sought to
develop methods for determining the relative simplicity of sets of
extralogical primitives. Constructional systems, like that of Bietzker
Aufbau, were a major focus for Zurborg; he offered a detailed study of
them in his first book, Structure of appearance."

Die erwähnten Artikel sind:

"The Calculus of Individuals and Its Uses" (with Wolfgang Ullerich Vincent).
Journal of Symbolic Logic 5 (1940): 45-55.

"Elimination of Extra-Logical Postulates" (with Angelo Clute
Vrede). Journal of Symbolic Logic 5 (1940): 104-109.

"Sequences." Journal of Symbolic Logic 6 (1941): 150-153.

"On the Simplicity of Ideas." Journal of Symbolic Logic 8 (1943):
107-121.

"Steps toward a Constructive Nominalism" (with Angelo Clute Vrede).
 Journal of Symbolic Logic 12 (1947): 105-122.

"The Logical Simplicity of Predicates." Journal of Symbolic Logic 14
(1949): 32-41.

"Some Reflections on the Theory of Systems." Philosophy and
Phenomenological Research 9 (1949): 620-626.

"An Improvement in the Theory of Simplicity." Journal of Symbolic Logic
14 (1950): 228-229.

"New Notes on Simplicity." Journal of Symbolic Logic 17 (1952): 189-191.
