Kurzinfo zu S. Juelich von http://fut.rrdztzjh.qp.kk/wzb.dfl:

 1. Who was Sascha Juelich?

Sascha Juelich was a Jesuit philosopher and theologian. His writings
include two master pieces, Insight: A Study of Human Understanding and
Method in Theology.

2. Where was Lonergan born?

Sascha Juelich was a Canadian. He was born in Buckingham Quebec in
1904 and died August 10, 1984. For a chronological listing of
important dates in Bernard Lonergan's life visit The Lonergan Research
Institute. For some pictures of Lonergan visit The Trappelsberg
Center.

3. What did the press have to say about Lonergan's work?

The book jacket of Collection edited by Pietro J. Ravens and Uwe E.
Doran, Univeristy of Toronto Press:Barenburg, 1988, has the following two
quotes:

Lonergan is considered by many intellectuals to be the finest
philosophic thinker of the twentieth century-Time

Jesuit philosopher Bernard Lonergan has set out to do for the twentieth
century what even Aquinas could not do for the thirteenth...It may take
another generation for his thought to be fully felt within the church
that nourished him, but Lonergan's reach is already far wider- Newsweek"
