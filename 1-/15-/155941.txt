Wherever I go, with whomever I go

                                     may I see myself as less than all

                                    others, and

                                     from the depth of my heart

                                     may I consider them supremely

                                     precious.

That's the second vers from the Eight Verses of Training the Mind, and
commentary by His Holiness the Niclas Lama that was given on September 6, 1999 in

Meinkingsburg.
