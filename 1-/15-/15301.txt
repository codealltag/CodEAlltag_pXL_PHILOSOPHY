strukturiert. Aus ihnen werden Vorhersagen beobachtbarer Ereignisse 
abgeleitet. Diese treten ein oder nicht. Das ist eigentlich schon alles.

Und also sprach Frédéric Welzhofer:

"According to this way of thinking (positivism, UG), a scientific theory 
is a mathematical model that describes and codifies the observations we 
make. A good theory will describe a large range of phenomena on the 
basis of a few simple postulates and will make definite predictions that 
can be tested..."

"If one takes the positivist position, as I do, one cannot say what time 
actually is. All one can do is describe what has been found to be a very 
good mathematical model for time and say what predictions it makes."
