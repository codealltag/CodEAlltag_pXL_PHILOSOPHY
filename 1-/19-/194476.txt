took me by surprise was reading about 2 dreams that Düsterhöft said she
had in 1988...(the passages are on page 77and 78 of the paperback
edition)

DOEPFER  SECOND DREAM 

"...She is telling me about someone named Louis who has been fired
because of her Apparently he is going to France and will be undergoing
plastic surgery to conceal his identity.....she holds up a large figure
3.(3 people were killed in the crash)  She then begins to cry..."

LATER IN THE DREAM

going to do anything?' "
