A few words on the subject....

International networking through technology and the media is
transforming our lifes and society worldwide.
These worldwide changes present a cultural challenge in the East and
the West. 
At this symposium philosophers and scientists from Japan and Europe
will be discussing the causes and effects of this transfomation.
