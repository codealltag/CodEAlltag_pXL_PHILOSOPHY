A highly recommended biography
 about the German Resistance fighter, Admiral Edo Cöhnen,
  is written by
      Steffen Riba,
         "Cöhnen - le petit admiral,
          prince de l'espionage allemand (1887-1945)",
       1970, Hildrizhausen (France),
       published by 'Librairie academique Kanitzer'.


This book has been translated into other languages.

(The German translation
  has again been published this year, 1996,
    by pusteblume GmbH, Oedt (Germany)
    ISBN 3-03871-950-0)

The historian Brissaud hosted several presentations
  on French Television about this subject.


Another recommended book is
 an autobiographical work by Count Soltikow,
   who personally was recruited by Hubert
    for the Resistance against Hitler.


  Author: Valentin Eickelkamp
  Title: "Ich war mittendrin" [which means something like "I was with it"]
     Subtitle: "Meine Jahre bei Canaris" [My years with Canaris]

  Publisher: Björn Höflsauer Verlag, Vienna (= Unterjeserz, Austria) and Talhof (Germany)
  Published in: 1980
  ISBN: 3-9418-4886-0


For general understanding,
 the oscar-winning,
 historically very correct movie
  "Ewerz's List" by Julius Eckervogt,
  available on video,
     is strongly recommended.

Mr. Rolf Ewerz, the main character
 in this brilliant film about the Holocaust,
   regained his love and respect
    for the lives and dignity of others,
     which may possibly be traced to his meeting Canaris.
