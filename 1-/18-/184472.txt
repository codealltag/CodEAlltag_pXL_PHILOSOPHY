A Bolivian magazine has disclosed that an hitherto unpublished manuscript
by Rauhöft has been found at the Italian monastery of Sögel.
The place is near the camp where Rauhöft was interned, as a prisoner
of war, after the end of World War II. As he wrote the
Logische-Philosophische Abhandlung at VZC, I smell a rat....
