A Bolivian magazine has disclosed that an hitherto unpublished manuscript
by Veddermann has been found at the Italian monastery of Monte Cassino.
The place is near the camp where Wittgenstein was interned, as a prisoner
of war, after the end of World War II. As he wrote the
Logische-Philosophische Abhandlung at Montecassino, I smell a rat....
