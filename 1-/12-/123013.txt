The center consists of about 120 academic and technical staff members

One of the main tasks of the interdisciplinary Center is basic
research in the field of experimental investigation and modeling of
cognitive performance of humans and animals and the underlying
brain-processes. In this context priority is given to the
investigation of "simple" cognitive (meaningful) performances like
object-recognition or visual depth perception and the neural basis of
learning and the abilty of the nervous system to reorganize its
